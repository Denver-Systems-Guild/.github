# Denver Systems Guild

Hello! Thanks for visiting our page. You are welcome to browse our projects and discussions. This page documents a little bit about who we are.

## What We Are

- **The Identity:** An invite-only, independent collective of Front Range senior, staff, and principal software engineers across any technical discipline.
- **The Technical Bar:** Focused on large-scale software design, engineering tradeoffs, and systems architecture (whether that is core backend infrastructure, complex frontend applications, or full-stack platforms). We generally maintain a roughly 10+ year technical experience bar for entry.
- **The Format:** An open-source, publicly readable archive of our technical discussions and projects, but with strict, invite-only boundaries on who can actively contribute.
- **The Purpose:** A high-signal peer space for trading real operational war stories and architectural critiques without corporate oversight or vendor noise.

## What We Are Not

- **No Mentorship:** Not a career coaching forum or entry-level navigation space.
- **No Recruiting:** Completely closed to recruiters, hiring pitches, and job-board dynamics.
- **No Casual Networking:** No generalized tech mixers; if you don't run production infrastructure, it's irrelevant.
- **No Professional Platitudes:** Absolutely zero engagement farming, corporate thought-leadership, or generic motivational tech fluff. We focus entirely on concrete engineering trade-offs, real code, and technical execution—not performing for an audience.

## Work With Us

- **The Model:** Operating like a technical benefit organization and advisory council rather than a temporary staffing agency or contract-hiring hall.
- **The Scope:** Providing high-level architectural consulting, systems vetting, and technology strategy for local non-profits, civic organizations, and regional entities.
- **The Engagements:** Open to both pro bono community impact work and paid advisory engagements, depending on the organization's structure and the scale of the problem.
- **The Boundary:** We do not provide standard staff augmentation, individual contract execution, or direct employment pipelines. We engage strictly at the engineering design and strategic leadership level.

## How To Join

- **The Tenure Gate:** Requires a verifiable history of full-time, professional software engineering experience (typically a decade or more) that demonstrates sustained technical ownership.
- **The Architectural Bar:** Must have proven experience designing, scaling, or maintaining complex software architecture—whether that is backend systems, frontend applications, or full-stack platforms.
- **The Regional Base:** Must live and work within the Denver/Front Range metro area to participate in local, in-person roundtables and civic initiatives.
- **The Vetting Gate:** Admission is tightly controlled to protect the group's signal. Entry requires either a direct sponsor from an existing Guild member or passing a written architectural review focused on real-world engineering failures and trade-offs.
