# Denver Systems Guild

Hello! Thanks for visiting our page. You are welcome to browse our projects and discussions. This page documents a little bit about who we are.

You might be looking for the [discussion page](https://github.com/orgs/Denver-Systems-Guild/discussions).

## What We Are

- **The Identity:** An invite-only, independent collective of Front Range senior, staff, and principal software engineers across any technical discipline.
- **The Technical Bar:** Focused on large-scale software design, engineering tradeoffs, and systems architecture (whether that is core backend infrastructure, complex frontend applications, or full-stack platforms). We generally maintain a roughly 10+ year technical experience bar for entry.
- **The Format:** An open-source, publicly readable archive of our technical discussions and projects, but with strict, invite-only boundaries on who can actively contribute.
- **The Purpose:** A high-signal peer space for trading real operational war stories and architectural critiques without corporate oversight or vendor noise.

## What We Are Not

- **No Mentorship:** Not a career coaching forum or entry-level navigation space.
- **No Recruiting:** Completely closed to recruiters, hiring pitches, and job-board dynamics.
- **No Casual Networking:** No generalized tech mixers; if you don't run production infrastructure, it's irrelevant.
- **No Professional Platitudes:** Absolutely zero engagement farming, corporate thought-leadership, or generic motivational tech fluff. We focus entirely on concrete engineering trade-offs, real code, and technical execution—not performing for an audience.

## How To Join

We are currently *invite only* via personal reference.
